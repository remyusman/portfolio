40 Days of Dating Poster

Every day for forty days, I met with another design student and noted what we talked about, how I felt, and the process of meeting. After those six weeks we each designed posters capturing the stress of changing schedules, forgetting to meet, and long text message chains. My poster below mashes up two of our designs to convey the confusion and stress felt throughout the project.
